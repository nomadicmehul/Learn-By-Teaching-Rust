# Learn-By-Teaching-Rust

We will derive an overview on how you can find interested people and get a learning group together to grow and keep your local community.

Thanks for your interest in contributing to [ Learn-By-Teaching-Rust ]! There are many ways to contribute. To get started, take a look at [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md).
